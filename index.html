Welcome

Thank you for visiting our website. Feel free to explore our site and learn about our community-welcoming cafe.

Whether you are craving caffeine in the form of coffee, would like a hot drink, delicious pastries, or a cozy, chill place to hang out, we are the cafe for you! We are community driven. All of our ingredients are locally sourced, meaning we have a menu that changes with the seasons. We do our best to reduce our carbon footprint by using 100% recyclable dishes and utensils for any take-out orders.

Reviews

We welcome any feed-back from our customers, whether you visited one time while in town or are a regular. A whole page is set up for these reviews, if you are considering visiting please take a look at our Customer Reviews.

Events

In our goal to be part of the community, we are constantly hosting events at our location. If you would like to have an event hosted with us or are interested in attending an open event here, please see our Hosted Events page.

Menu

Want to figure out what to order before traveling to our location? Or just curious what we offer? Check out our Menu page.
