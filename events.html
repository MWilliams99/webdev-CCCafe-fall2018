Events

We gladly host events at our location. If you are interested in having your event hosted with us, please contact us. If you are interested in attending an event, check out what is coming up:

  • Halloween Celebration

    Tuesday, October 30: Come join us in celebrating an early Halloween. Creepy-crawly Halloween inspired pastries and treats will be served. A costume contest will be held with prizes for the top 2 winners.

  • Band Fundraiser

    Friday, November 9: The local highschool will be holding a fundraiser for their band, we will be selling baked goods which all profits will go to this fundraiser.

  • Thanksgiving Celebration

    Sunday, November 21: Join us in celebrating Thanksgiving! This kid-friendly event will include Thanksgiving stories read to the children, arts-and-crafts themed for Thanksgiving, and Thanksgiving themed desserts.

Make sure to stop by if you see an event you would like to attend! You can get directions using Google Maps.
